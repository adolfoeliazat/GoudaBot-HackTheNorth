Your Personal Restaurant Finder

GoudaBot uses Bots for Messenger to help you find great restaurants in seconds

Visit The Page

GoudaBot utilizes Facebook’s Bots for Messenger alongside Yelp’s API to create a personal assistant capable of finding you restaurants that fit your needs. You have the ability to find restaurants based on their ratings, their proximity to your location, or a general best match. You can also search restaurants up to a maximum radius.

Find Restaurants Easily.

Quickly search for restaurants in a given city for a list of various restaurant suggestions to choose from. Once found, you have the option of visiting the restaurant’s Yelp page for more information or contacting the restaurant directly by phone to make reservations or anything else you wish to do.

Refine Search Results.

In addition to searching based on general city, you may also search for restaurants based on specific terms or restaurant types (i.e. Italian). You may also search for restaurants based on how well they match your terms, their proximity to your location, or based on their overall Yelp ratings. If proximity to a city doesn’t work for you, you may also search them based on a maximum radius from your current position.

Get Support Instantly.

GoudaBot provides multiple forms of support in order to ensure the features can be easily used. By using the menu located in the bottom corner, you may trigger a help message with some basic information or a format message that explains the basic format of message requests. Also, once you are provided with restaurant results, you are asked if you wish to see additional results in the even that none of the results work for you.
