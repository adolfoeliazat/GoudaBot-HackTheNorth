Privacy Policy

GoudaBot doesn't store any of your information, in fact, we don't even have a database that is connected to our service. Temporary information is stored in memory while the app is running, however once complete, this information no longer exists unless re-entered. We also utilize the Yelp API in order to provide you with the results, meaning that the data you enter when messaging the bot (type of restaurant, location, etc.) is sent to Yelp, so as a result, you would be subject to their terms of use and/or privacy policy as well.
